OUR STORY

Nurturing the seeds of peace and development.

Daima Initiatives for Peace and Development (DIPAD) is a community-driven catalyst committed to fostering sustainable justice and inclusive growth across Kenya.

Our Mission

"To empower communities through sustainable peace-building initiatives and socio-economic programs that foster long-term stability and dignity."

Our Journey

2011

Founding in Nairobi

DIPAD was born out of a grassroots movement to address urban inequality and provide peace-building workshops during critical election periods.

2017

Expanding Horizons

Recognizing the interconnectedness of peace and prosperity, we expanded into agricultural development and youth vocational training across the Rift Valley.

Today

A National Catalyst

With over 15 regional offices, DIPAD now serves as a key consultant for sustainable development and social justice policies across the East African region.
